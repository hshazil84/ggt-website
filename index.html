Our Services

Logistics Solutions Designed for Modern Trade

Four specialised divisions, one integrated partner.

01

Sea Freight (LCL & FCL)

Whether you are shipping a few pallets or a fleet of containers, our sea freight solutions offer the perfect balance between cost and transit time.

  • FCL (Full Container Load) — Dedicated 20ft and 40ft containers for maximum security and efficiency from Jebel Ali Port.
  • LCL (Less than Container Load) — Consolidation services — pay only for the space you use. Ideal for growing businesses.
  • Coverage: Asia, Indian Subcontinent, Europe, East Africa, and beyond.
  • Full documentation: Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin, customs declaration.
02

Air Freight

When time is the most critical factor, our air freight services provide rapid transit to and from any major global hub via Dubai International Airport.

  • Priority handling via DXB — one of the world's busiest cargo hubs.
  • Time-definite delivery for urgent or high-value consignments.
  • AWB management and UAE customs clearance included.
  • 24/7 real-time tracking through the Gulhaaru Shipper Portal.
03

General Sourcing & Trading

We act as your extended procurement arm — identifying reputable manufacturers, negotiating terms, and managing quality control end-to-end.

  • Identification of global manufacturers across key sourcing markets.
  • Price negotiation and commercial terms management on your behalf.
  • Pre-shipment quality control inspection and verification.
  • End-to-end management from purchase order to warehouse door.
04

Specialised Pharmaceutical Division

Navigating the healthcare supply chain requires precision. Gulhaaru specializes in compliant pharmaceutical sourcing and distribution across the UAE and GCC.

  • Sourcing of high-demand pharmaceutical and healthcare products.
  • Wholesale distribution and licensed reselling within UAE and GCC.
  • Strict adherence to UAE Ministry of Health (MOH) standards.
  • Cold-chain logistics — temperature-controlled storage and transport.
  • Compliance documentation: health certificates, MOH approvals, COAs.
